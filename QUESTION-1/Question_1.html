1. WHAT ARE HTML ATTRIBUTES? EXPLAIN WITH TWO EXAMPLES.


  DEFINITION:

  HTML Attributes are used to add more information about the tag.
  HTML Attributes are nothing, but additional information about the tag.
  It's always define in the opening tag.

  EXAMPLE:

  1. <img src=" " alt=" " />
        Here,
        'src' and 'alt' both are the Attributes of <img> tag.
        src = URL[Uniform Resource Locator] of Image.
        alt = alternative information about the Image.(for some reason When user cannot see the image)

  2. <a href=" " target=" "> Google </a>
        Here,
        'href' and 'target' both are the Attributes of <a> tag.
        href = Hyperlink Refrence.
        target = specify where you want to open your Hyperlink.
