2. WHAT IS A MARQUEE IN HTML? EXPLAIN WITH TWO EXAMPLES.


  DEFINITION:

  marquee tag is used to create scrollable text and images.
  It scrolls either 'Horizontally' [left to right OR right to left] OR 'Vertically' [top to bottom OR bottom to top]
  marquee tag is deprecated in HTML5.
